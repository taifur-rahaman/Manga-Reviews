## Overview

*Prices Depreciated, and I Became the World’s Richest Person* follows the story of a protagonist who awakens in a world where the value of money has drastically changed—all prices have depreciated by a million times. However, his personal funds remain completely unchanged. Overnight, he instantly becomes the wealthiest individual globally. The manhua explores how he navigates this new reality and utilizes his sudden, immense fortune.

## Story & World

*(To be updated as I continue reading)*

The premise is straightforward but offers a lot of comedic and slice-of-life potential. Waking up to find that everyday items now cost fractions of a cent while keeping your previous bank balance intact is the ultimate wish-fulfillment scenario. The story focuses on the bizarre situations that arise when someone has functionally infinite wealth compared to the rest of the world.
