## Overview

*(To be updated as I continue reading)*

"Beyond Myriad Peoples" (often known as "Above Ten Thousand People") is a comedy cultivation manhua about a protagonist who is an unmatched powerhouse but believes he is a mere mortal without any cultivation base. He lives a "simple" life, unaware that his every action shakes the world and that the "items" he creates are supreme artifacts.

## Story & World

*(To be updated as I continue reading)*

The story revolves around the misunderstanding between the overpowered main character and the cultivation world. While he thinks he's just running a small shop/gym and trying to survive among cultivators, the most powerful experts in the world see him as a reclusive, god-like senior. The humor comes from the gap between his perceived weakness and his actual, terrifying strength.

## Characters

*(To be updated as I continue reading)*

**Lin Fan (Protagonist)** - The "mortal" main character who is actually a supreme existence. He is clueless about his own power and treats legendary beasts and artifacts as common pets and tools.
