## Overview

*(To be updated as I continue reading)*

*It Turns Out I've Been Invincible for a Long Time* follows Ye Changqing, who spent five years journeying around the world believing he had no cultivation talent whatsoever. Rather than chasing glory, he settled near a sect in a small town and opened a humble store. What he didn't know — and what everyone else around him could clearly see — is that his store had quietly become the holy land of all righteous individuals, the dog he casually raised is the feared demon king, and Ye Changqing himself is regarded by everyone as a Supreme Master.

## Story & World

*(To be updated as I continue reading)*

The story is set in a classic Xianxia cultivation world full of sects, demons, and immortals. The comedy and appeal come entirely from the gap between Ye Changqing's blissfully unaware self-perception as an ordinary merchant and the reality that every being around him is in awe or terror of his unknowing supremacy. The business-building slice-of-life angle mixed with accidental supremacy gives it a very distinctive flavour.

## Characters

*(To be updated as I continue reading)*
