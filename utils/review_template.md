## Cover Image Generation

When generating cover images, use prompts that:
- Match the art style (Manga/Manhwa/Manhua specific)
- Include the title in the image
- Reflect the genre and tone of the series
- Feature key characters or themes
- Use vibrant, eye-catching colors
